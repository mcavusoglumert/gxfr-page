PERSONAL LIVES

The trio hails from Buffalo, New York. Westside Gunn & Conway are brothers and Benny is their cousin. The three of them have been convicted on different charges. Conway suffers from Bell's palsy.

PERSONAL STYLES

Conway the Machine's delivery is cold and grimy. He gives you goosebumps, never plays around.

Benny the Butcher gives you that straight talk, one liners every-day. Brings the coke-rap game a different flavour.
